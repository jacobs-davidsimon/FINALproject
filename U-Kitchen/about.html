About U-Kitchen

The UNKLAB (Universitas Klabat) canteen—sometimes referred to as the "School Labor Canteen" in official reports—is a practical, open-air facility located within the university grounds that serves snacks and meals to students, staff, and nearby schoolchildren. Layout & Seating: It features a simple, un-air-conditioned open structure with several food stalls and communal plastic seating—ideal for quick meals and casual conversations. Ambiance: During meal times, it’s lively and bustling, with students chatting and networking over trays of food.
